About us

360 Security Training is a UK vocational training provider. Our approach blends hands-on practice with scenario-based learning to ensure real-world confidence. We currently focus on First Aid qualifications and deliver ACT Awareness/A CT Security via UK Protect.

We work with organisations and individuals across sectors; on-site delivery is available.
